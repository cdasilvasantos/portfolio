Project Overview

Luxcore Steakhouse, a fictional business brought to life through a dynamic website created using Next.js, stands as the centerpiece of our IS 218 Class Final Project. In a collaborative effort with Michael Daniels, we seamlessly integrated the power of Next.js to deliver a smooth and responsive user experience. To ensure the reliability and robustness of our application, we implemented Playwright testing, guaranteeing a stable and error-free platform for users. The website's aesthetic and functionality were elevated with the integration of the Chakra Component Library, providing a visually appealing and cohesive design. Additionally, we enhanced user engagement by incorporating Mailchimp for efficient communication and leveraged the insights provided by Google Analytics to track and analyze user interactions, ensuring continuous optimization of Luxcore Steakhouse's online presence.
